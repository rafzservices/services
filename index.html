Since we do not collect any user data:

3. Children’s Privacy

Because our app does not collect any personal data, it is compliant with child privacy protection laws, including the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

4. Security

Although no personal data is collected, we still implement safeguards in our app’s design to prevent unintended data collection and ensure secure operations.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Since we do not collect any personal data, changes will typically relate to app functionality or legal compliance.
